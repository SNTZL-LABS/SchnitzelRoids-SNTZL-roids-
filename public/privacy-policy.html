Privacy Policy for SNTZL-ROIDS

1. Information We Collect

The only information we collect about you is:

  • Your own fictitious in-game name you have provided to us
  • The optional Ethereum address you have provided to us
  • Server analytics might be collected (anonymized) to optimize coding performance

No other personal information gets collected, stored or sold

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Display your in-game name
  • Process prize transactions (if you've provided an Ethereum address)

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. If you've provided an Ethereum address, please note that transactions on the Ethereum blockchain are public and may be visible to others.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the limited information we collect. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.
